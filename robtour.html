I am just a little upset here! Rob is reportedly frustrated with America and I don't blame him! Supposedly a body double was used in the new U.S. video for Angels because Rob was too "fat." Since the beginning he has been marketed as an "ex-boyband member" appealing to the Backstreet Boys, teen crowd when he has a more original and larger appeal. His past club tour was did not do as well as hoped and EMI has not officially released Angels! What can be done about this? Well, I think Rob needs a bit of encouragement for one. May 14, 1999

Here's Rob'sU.S. Billboard Chart Standings and album salesas of May 13, 1999: He's only sold 18,800 albums (not that great so far) and will be ranked #85 on Billboard. May 4, 1999

Today's the day!The Ego has officially Landed in the U.S.Rob's album came out today! I'm currently scanning the pictures from the insert to put up on this site. The cover is great, but I'm sure you've all seen it by now!

Tonight he also had a live webcast from New York City House of Blues I was away all evening unfortunately and missed it. Check and see if the concert was archived!

Big fun! An online store of Rob goodiesopened today! Go check it out!

Rob also did hisnetwork television appearance last night on Dave Letterman. This is the first time I have ever really seen Rob interviewed, but he was hilarious! He was very excited, and definitely a bit nervous too. He sang Millenium, of course, changing it up a little in the bridge to say "Dave Letterman" instead of "Millenium." He was rumored to be on Saturday Night Live sometime this month, but since the season is soon ending, it looks unlikely.
